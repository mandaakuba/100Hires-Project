# AI-Powered SEO & AEO: Expert Sources

This document contains the curated list of 10 high-signal experts selected for the Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) playbook research. These practitioners were chosen because they actively build and execute AI SEO strategies, rather than just theorizing.

## 1. Kevin Indig (Growth Advisor, ex-Shopify/G2)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Interview (The Search Session)
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AqAoKGftsSE&t=1722s
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Indig provides a macro-architectural view of AI search. His insights reveal how LLMs function as sophisticated online reputation management surfaces, emphasizing that offline visibility and brand entities are now critical ranking factors for agentic search.

## 2. Sam Oh (Ahrefs)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Video
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gReszNnykpg
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Oh delivers a data-backed framework for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) based on 25 million AI overviews. He proves that semantic HTML structure (tree walking algorithms), content freshness for RAG models, and branded mentions outperform traditional backlinks.

## 3. Bernard Huang (Clearscope)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Webinar
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Ldce9z_ZuM
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Huang offers a highly tactical AEO playbook. He decodes the "AI validation layer," explaining how LLMs perform live web searches to fact-check data. He advocates for prompt tracking over traditional keyword research to intercept AI agents.

## 4. John-Henry Scherck (Growth Plays)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Interview (Goldenhour)
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOsLXyIZ4Og
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Scherck tackles the "Great Decoupling" of impressions vs. clicks. He anchors the strategy in building trust for B2B SaaS, proving that focusing on deep "Jobs to Be Done" content drives pipeline even when overall top-of-funnel traffic drops.

## 5. Ethan Smith (Graphite)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Interview
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xAXy8xz-hao
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Smith addresses the "sea of sameness" in LLM outputs. He provides a blueprint for strict information gain—injecting unique metadata, leveraging off-site user-generated content, and optimizing video abstractions to force AI citations.

## 6. Eric Siu (Leveling Up)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Video
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmKPtCUZlCE
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Siu expands the focus to "Search Everywhere Optimization." His methodology covers using AI to automate the heavy lifting of technical SEO, such as programmatic internal linking and identifying high-intent long-tail query gaps.

## 7. Neil Patel (NP Digital)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Video
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tMBdA2gkXgk
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Patel maps the evolution of E-E-A-T into the AI era. He emphasizes that AI models are scanners, requiring content to be explicitly formatted with short paragraphs, bullets, and schema markups to act as a nutrition label for LLMs.

## 8. Nico (AI Ranking)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Video (Complete SEO Course)
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ClGDDrsc_Xs
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026
* **Annotation:** Nico provides systematic workflows for integrating AI into localized and niche SEO. His focus is on matching search intent through structured content clusters and executing rapid programmatic content generation.

## 9. Vasco (Vasco's SEO Tips)
* **Source Type:** YouTube Video (Complete AI SEO Course)
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cD5fvD3f8Mc
* **Date Analyzed:** April 2026

## 10. Aleyda Solis (Orainti)
* **Source Type:** YouTube / LinkedIn Analysis
* **Link:** https://www.youtube.com/@AleydaSolis
